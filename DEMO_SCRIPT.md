# Bright Smile Dental — 90-Second Demo Script

Use this for your Loom recording. Hit each beat in order. Total runtime: ~90 seconds.

---

## Opening (0:00–0:10)
> "Let me show you a full-stack dental clinic website I built — complete with an AI chatbot, online booking, and admin dashboard. Everything a modern clinic needs."

**Screen:** Homepage loading

---

## Homepage (0:10–0:25)
> "The homepage opens with the clinic's brand, trust signals, and two clear CTAs. Scroll down and you get services, the team, social proof, and a final booking call-to-action."

**Action:** Slowly scroll the homepage — pause briefly on the hero, services grid, and testimonials carousel.

---

## Service Detail (0:25–0:40)
> "Every service has its own detail page with descriptions, what to expect, transparent pricing, and FAQs. This is the content that converts."

**Action:** Click on "Dental Implants" from the services page. Show the pricing breakdown.

---

## Booking Flow (0:40–1:00)
> "The booking form is three steps — service selection, date and time picker, then patient info with validation. Hit confirm and you get a real confirmation number."

**Action:** Go to /book → pick a service → pick a date and time slot → fill in name/email/phone → confirm. Show the confirmation screen.

---

## AI Chatbot (1:00–1:20)
> "Now the selling point — Brighty, the AI dental assistant. It knows everything about the clinic: hours, pricing, insurance, services. Ask it anything."

**Action:** Click the chat bubble. Type: "What does teeth whitening cost?"
Then type: "Do you accept Delta Dental?"
Then click "Book an appointment" chip.

> "It even captures leads — when a patient says they want to book, it collects their info and saves it to the admin dashboard."

---

## Admin Dashboard (1:20–1:30)
> "Dentists log in to the admin portal and see today's appointments, pending confirmations, and chatbot leads — all in one clean dashboard."

**Action:** Go to /admin/login → log in → show the dashboard → click one appointment row to show detail panel → click Appointments → show the Inquiries page with a chatbot conversation.

---

## Closing (1:30 end)
> "Everything is deployed on Vercel, backed by Supabase, and all the clinic data lives in a single file — so I can clone this and launch for any dental client in hours. That's the system."

---

## Tips for Recording
- Use a 1440px browser window for best visuals
- Record at 60fps for smooth scrolling
- Zoom browser to 90% for more content visible
- Have the chat pre-typed to avoid spelling mistakes on camera
- Mute notifications before recording
